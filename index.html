OVERVIEW

Introducing AstraX – The Multi-Purpose Figma Template

AstraX is a powerful and versatile Figma template designed to cater to a wide range of industries, from business and finance to AI solutions and creative agencies. Whether you're launching a startup, running an e-learning platform, or managing an event, AstraX provides a comprehensive design system with pre-built layouts tailored to various professional needs.

Skip the tedious work of designing from scratch—AstraX streamlines the process with intuitive and fully customizable components, helping designers, developers, and business owners bring their projects to life effortlessly.

Who is AstraX for?

  • Designers, skip creating from scratch those boring web elements along with every dashboard. Use these templates to concentrate on important things, not on pixel routine.
  • Developers, this design library allows you to learn or enhance web design skills. Jump into a process, where you only need to drag and fit a block to build good-looking pages.
  • Managesrs & Owners, these templates are ready to be published for teams and provide an opportunity to quickstart massive prototyping immediately.

Key Features

  • ✅ 01 Figma File – Organized, structured, and easy to navigate.
  • ✅ 27 Completed Websites – Covering a variety of industries and digital solutions.
